Rodz Custom Construction LLC was founded with a single conviction: that great construction isn't just about the finished product — it's about the trust, communication, and craftsmanship that gets you there.

Founder Mr. Rodriguez "Rod" Castillo began his career ... before launching Rodz Custom Construction in 2026.
